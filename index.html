Garena Company History

Garena is a Singapore-based company founded in 2009, part of Sea Ltd. It became globally famous due to Free Fire and other major games. It publishes online and mobile games worldwide, including League of Legends (SEA), Arena of Valor, and Call of Duty Mobile (Garena edition). Garena’s vision is to provide top-quality gaming experiences to players all over the world.
